8 kilometer

Run

This 3 kilometer run is short enough to complete during a study break and features garden and street scenery. Below, suggested starting and ending points are marked. The suggested route, which winds down Sidgwick Avenue, Silver Street, turns on Trumpington passing Kings College Chapel and the Fellows Garden is in the "Suggested Route" drop down section.

What Surface? Road/Grass Run
Which Colleges is it good for? King's, St Catherine's, Corpus Christi, Trinty Hall, Clare, Wolfson
Highlights Grancester Meadows, River
